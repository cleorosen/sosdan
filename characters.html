Haruhi

Haruhi Suzumiya

Japanese: 涼宮 ハルヒ (Suzumiya Haruhi)

Role: Brigade Chief and Founder

Class: 1-5 (First Season) / 2-5 (Second Season)

Haruhi is the eccentric and energetic founder of the SOS Brigade. She is bored with normal human life and seeks out supernatural phenomena and figures such as aliens, time travelers, and espers.

Click to reveal spoiler

Haruhi is unknowingly a god-like entity with the power to change reality according to her desires. Her emotional state can drastically affect the world around her, creating closed spaces when she becomes frustrated or bored.

Kyon

Kyon

Role: Narrator and Sole Normal Human

Class: 1-5 (First Season) / 2-5 (Second Season)

Kyon is the narrator and main protagonist of the series. Despite his cynical and sarcastic nature, he is the only normal human in the group and serves as the audience surrogate. He is the only person who can talk back to Haruhi without facing severe consequences.

Click to reveal spoiler

Despite being "normal," Kyon is actually Haruhi's chosen one, and has the ability to influence her more than anyone else. Some theories suggest he may have powers of his own that he is unaware of.

Yuki

Yuki Nagato

Japanese: 長門 有希 (Nagato Yuki)

Role: Literature Club Member, SOS Brigade Member

Class: 1-5 (First Season) / 2-5 (Second Season)

Yuki is a quiet, reserved girl who is always reading in the club room. She rarely shows emotion and speaks very little. She is the only original member of the Literature Club which the SOS Brigade took over.

Click to reveal spoiler

Yuki is actually a humanoid interface created by the Data Overmind, an alien entity that exists as data. She possesses immense data manipulation powers, allowing her to alter reality, create shields, and fight at superhuman levels.

Mikuru

Mikuru Asahina

Japanese: 朝比奈 みくる (Asahina Mikuru)

Role: SOS Brigade Member, "Mascot"

Class: 2-A (First Season) / 3-A (Second Season)

Mikuru is a shy, timid girl who was forcibly recruited into the SOS Brigade by Haruhi to serve as the "cute mascot." She is often made to wear various costumes by Haruhi, much to her embarrassment. Despite her timid nature, she is kind-hearted and cares for her fellow club members.

Click to reveal spoiler

Mikuru is actually a time traveler from the future who was sent back to observe Haruhi. Her mission is classified, and she is often unable to divulge information due to creating time paradoxes. There are two versions of her in the timeline: the current Mikuru and her future self.

Koizumi

Itsuki Koizumi

Japanese: 古泉 一樹 (Koizumi Itsuki)

Role: SOS Brigade Member, Transfer Student

Class: 1-9 (First Season) / 2-9 (Second Season)

Koizumi is a transfer student who joins the brigade shortly after its formation. He is always smiling and speaks in a polite, somewhat cryptic manner. He appears to go along with all of Haruhi's plans without complaint and often explains situations to Kyon.

Click to reveal spoiler

Koizumi is an esper who works for the "Agency," an organization dedicated to monitoring Haruhi and combating the closed spaces and celestials she creates when emotionally unstable. He, along with other espers, gains his powers within these closed spaces.

Other Notable Characters

  • Shamisen: A calico cat that gains the ability to speak during the filming of the brigade's movie
  • Adult Mikuru: Future version of Mikuru who occasionally appears to guide events
  • Yasumi Watahashi: A mysterious character who appears in later stories
  • Kuyou Suou: A representative of another alien faction, the Sky Canopy Dominion
  • Fujiwara: A rogue time traveler opposed to Mikuru's faction
  • Sasaki: Kyon's middle school friend who may have powers similar to Haruhi
  • Tsuruya: Mikuru's energetic and cheerful friend with distinctive green hair and a loud laugh
  • Kyon's Sister: Kyon's energetic younger sister who admires Haruhi and sometimes visits the club
  • Computer Research Society President: Forced to give his computer to the SOS Brigade after being framed
  • Taniguchi: Kyon's friend and classmate who once dated Haruhi briefly
  • Kunikida: Another of Kyon's friends who often appears alongside Taniguchi
  • Emiri Kimidori: Another humanoid interface like Nagato who appears occasionally
